Privacy Policy

Meal Prep Tally is an offline-first app. All data you enter — including entries, dates, and notes — is stored locally on your device using Apple's local storage APIs. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. The app makes no network requests of its own; the only network activity is Apple's own StoreKit purchase processing when you buy the optional Pro upgrade, which is handled entirely by Apple.

Because all data stays on your device, we have no access to it and cannot see, back up, or recover it if you delete the app. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes your local data.
